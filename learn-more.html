About PrivacyGuard Site Safety

PrivacyGuard helps you identify potentially unsafe websites.

Understanding Alerts:

  • Green Appears safe based on our analysis or your previous whitelisting.
  • Yellow Uses HTTP or has suspicious features. You may proceed, mark it as safe, or block it.
  • Red High risk (e.g., phishing). Proceed with extreme caution or block it.

User Actions:

  • Mark as Safe: Add this site to your personal whitelist.
  • Proceed Anyway: Continue to the site despite warnings.
  • Block Site: Prevent further access to this site.
  • Learn More: You're already here!

Your browsing data is processed locally on your device to protect your privacy.
